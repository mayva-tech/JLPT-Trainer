JLPT N2 Shopping Vocabulary #1
Supermarket • Shopping
========================================

1. 品切れ（しなぎれ）— out of stock
   Phrase: 品切れの商品
   Phrase meaning: out-of-stock product
   Sentence: この商品は品切れです。
   Sentence meaning: This product is out of stock.

2. 在庫（ざいこ）— stock
   Phrase: 在庫の確認
   Phrase meaning: checking stock
   Sentence: 在庫を確認します。
   Sentence meaning: I'll check the stock.

3. 割引（わりびき）— discount
   Phrase: 20パーセント割引
   Phrase meaning: 20 percent discount
   Sentence: この商品は割引中です。
   Sentence meaning: This product is on discount.

4. 値上がり（ねあがり）— price increase
   Phrase: 食品の値上がり
   Phrase meaning: rise in food prices
   Sentence: 野菜が値上がりしました。
   Sentence meaning: Vegetable prices have gone up.

5. 賞味期限（しょうみきげん）— best-before date
   Phrase: 賞味期限の表示
   Phrase meaning: best-before date label
   Sentence: 賞味期限を確認してください。
   Sentence meaning: Please check the best-before date.

6. 消費期限（しょうひきげん）— use-by date
   Phrase: 消費期限が近い
   Phrase meaning: the use-by date is soon
   Sentence: 消費期限は明日です。
   Sentence meaning: The use-by date is tomorrow.

7. 日用品（にちようひん）— daily necessities
   Phrase: 日用品売り場
   Phrase meaning: daily necessities section
   Sentence: 日用品は二階にあります。
   Sentence meaning: Daily necessities are on the second floor.

8. まとめ買い（まとめがい）— bulk buying
   Phrase: 水のまとめ買い
   Phrase meaning: buying water in bulk
   Sentence: 水をまとめ買いしました。
   Sentence meaning: I bought water in bulk.

9. 返品（へんぴん）— return
   Phrase: 商品の返品
   Phrase meaning: returning a product
   Sentence: この商品を返品したいです。
   Sentence meaning: I'd like to return this product.

10. レシート（れしーと）— receipt
    Phrase: 買い物のレシート
    Phrase meaning: shopping receipt
    Sentence: レシートをください。
    Sentence meaning: Please give me a receipt.
