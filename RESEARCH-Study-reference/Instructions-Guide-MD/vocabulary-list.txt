JLPT N2 Daily Life Vocabulary #1
Apartment • Grocery • Weather
========================================

── Apartment ──────────────────────────

1. 家賃（やちん）— rent
   Phrase: 家賃を払う
   Phrase meaning: to pay rent
   Sentence: 毎月、家賃を銀行振込で払っています。
   Sentence meaning: Every month I pay rent by bank transfer.

2. 契約（けいやく）— contract; agreement
   Phrase: 契約を結ぶ
   Phrase meaning: to sign a contract
   Sentence: アパートの契約を来週結ぶ予定です。
   Sentence meaning: I'm planning to sign the apartment contract next week.

3. 修理（しゅうり）— repair; fix
   Phrase: 修理を依頼する
   Phrase meaning: to request a repair
   Sentence: エアコンが壊れたので、修理を依頼しました。
   Sentence meaning: The air conditioner broke, so I requested a repair.

4. 鍵（かぎ）— key; lock
   Phrase: 鍵をかける
   Phrase meaning: to lock (a door)
   Sentence: 外出するときは必ず鍵をかけてください。
   Sentence meaning: Please always lock the door when you go out.

── Grocery ────────────────────────────

5. 割引（わりびき）— discount
   Phrase: 割引を適用する
   Phrase meaning: to apply a discount
   Sentence: 会員カードを見せると割引が適用されます。
   Sentence meaning: Show your membership card and a discount will be applied.

6. 消費期限（しょうひきげん）— expiry date; use-by date
   Phrase: 消費期限を確認する
   Phrase meaning: to check the expiry date
   Sentence: 買う前に必ず消費期限を確認してください。
   Sentence meaning: Please always check the expiry date before buying.

7. 袋（ふくろ）— bag; sack
   Phrase: 袋に入れる
   Phrase meaning: to put in a bag
   Sentence: レジ袋は有料なので、マイバッグを持ってきました。
   Sentence meaning: Plastic bags cost money, so I brought my own bag.

── Weather ────────────────────────────

8. 湿度（しつど）— humidity
   Phrase: 湿度が高い
   Phrase meaning: humidity is high
   Sentence: 梅雨の季節は湿度が高くて不快に感じます。
   Sentence meaning: During the rainy season, the high humidity feels uncomfortable.

9. 予報（よほう）— forecast
   Phrase: 天気予報を確認する
   Phrase meaning: to check the weather forecast
   Sentence: 出かける前に天気予報を確認する習慣があります。
   Sentence meaning: I make it a habit to check the weather forecast before going out.

10. 避難（ひなん）— evacuation
    Phrase: 避難を指示する
    Phrase meaning: to issue an evacuation order
    Sentence: 台風が接近したため、市から避難の指示が出ました。
    Sentence meaning: As the typhoon approached, the city issued an evacuation order.
