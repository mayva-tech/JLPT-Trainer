# My biggest recommendation

I would **not** make videos simply titled:

> N2 Vocabulary #1

Instead, I'd use highly searchable titles such as:

* JLPT N2 Daily Life Vocabulary | Grocery & Shopping
* JLPT N2 Apartment Vocabulary
* JLPT N2 Weather Vocabulary
* JLPT N2 Train & Transportation Vocabulary
* JLPT N2 Hospital Vocabulary
* JLPT N2 Convenience Store Vocabulary

These are more likely to match what learners are actively searching for, and they also create a library of focused lessons rather than a long numbered sequence.

Because all of these draw from the same database, you can later regenerate the content into different formats—study lessons, listening-only videos, shadowing practice, quizzes, and mixed review sessions—without rewriting the vocabulary itself.
